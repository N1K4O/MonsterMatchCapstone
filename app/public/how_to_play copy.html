How to play MonsterMatch


Game Structure

When finding people to party with have them select a character and instruct them to dress up as that character for the party.

Have them look over their character’s lore and secret goals beforehand so they are familiar with it.

Once everyone arrives and you are ready to play, have the crypt keeper set the scene and start the “speed dating portion” Have each person partner up for 3-minute-long rounds to find out information about everyone else and Papa Victor.

After time is up have the players switch partners and then repeat the process until everyone has partnered with each other once

After that is the trivia portion, round up all the players and click the trivia button located under the how to play button.

From there read off as many trivia questions as you want, in whatever order you want, one at a time to the room. Players may not answer questions about themselves.

When a player gets a question right, they get a candy corn (real or imaginary).

Whoever has the most candy corn at the end wins. In the event of a tie, you decide how to handle that.
