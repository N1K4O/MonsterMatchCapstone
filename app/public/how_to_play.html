How to play MonsterMatch

Game Structure

The game will advance through several rounds in the order listed below. The first two games are know as the 'setup games' through which the final rounds will be influenced.

  1. Speed Dating (Three minutes per date)
  2. Trivia (Ten Questions)
  3. Ancestors Prophecy
  4. Murder Mystery 

Setup Games

Speed Date Goals

  • Talk Between Characters
  • Find Out Information regarding them
  • See who most matches what your "soul mate" really is
  • 3 minute "dates" to gather information

Trivia Goals

  • After the dates have passed, trivia questions will be prompted by the "Crypt Keeper" (Group Leader)
  • People that answer correctly will gain candy corns (points)
  • After the round of questions is over, candy corns will be counted and the half that has the least will be eliminated and become ghosts

Ancestor's Prophecy

The losers from the trivia game will be prompted with the next instructions. They will vote on a few possible options for the party members

  • Murderer
  • Angel
  • Lost Soul
  • Lost Soul
  • Grim Reaper

The story themes will be prompted to them. The choice of the theme will be voted by the ghosts. Ghosts will then set up the "Prophecy" and choose from several options what the story of the murder mystery is

Secrets will need to be kept by them for the mystery to stay hidden

The Murder Mystery Phase

  • Before the start, the Crypt Keeper will narrate the prophecy that was written by the ancestors
  • Every player (non ghost) will be given instructions and lore based on the story of the prophecy
  • 6 rounds
    • Each round will begin with the player with the most candy corns asking the first questions
    • Rounds consist of 2 phases
      1. The Conversation Phase
        • Players ask questions to the group or individuals that need to be answered (Group questions cost candy corn)
      2. Action Phase
        • After the conversation phase, the players will be able to perform an action based on their role
  • Ghosts will be able to silence questions/actions by spending candy corn
  • Each character may lie to a question by using their candy corn (Otherwise they need to speak the truth)

Character Roles

  • Murderer
    • Goal: Find out who the Angel is and murder them
  • Lost Soul (2)
    • Goal: Find their soulmate and match together before the Murderer kills the Angel
  • Angel
    • Goal: Make sure that the Lost Souls match and to not get killed by the Murderer
  • Grim Reaper
    • Goal: Find out who the Murderer is and reap their soul
  • Ghost
    • Goal: To keep all other players in a state of purgatory, just as they are
    • Wins if no one else has won at the end of the game
